Methodology & caveats

2026 values come from Cars.com nationwide asking medians, cross-checked against recent Bring a Trailer sold prints; the adopted figure leans to sold levels (asking × a per-car factor). 2020 baselines and mid-years are reconstructed from documented market lows and the 2021-22 run-up / 2023 wobble / 2024-26 surge — treat the 6-yr % as ±5-8 pts. Projections use momentum-aware CAGRs by each car's place on the modern-classic curve, drivetrain desirability, scarcity, and momentum; bull/bear bands convey uncertainty and are nominal. Maintenance = routine servicing + amortized wear (997 ≈$3k, 458 ≈$5k, F12 ≈$6k, 812s ≈$7k/yr); excludes insurance, storage, fuel, and major repairs. S&P 500 / AAPL are June levels (split-adjusted), not projected. Not investment advice.
